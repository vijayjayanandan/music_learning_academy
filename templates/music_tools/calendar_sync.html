How to Add

Google Calendar

  1. Go to Google Calendar Settings
  2. Click "Add other calendars" > "From URL"
  3. Paste the iCal feed URL above
  4. Click "Add Calendar"

Apple Calendar

  1. Open Calendar app
  2. File > New Calendar Subscription
  3. Paste the URL and click Subscribe

Outlook

  1. Go to Calendar > Add Calendar > From Internet
  2. Paste the URL
  3. Click OK
