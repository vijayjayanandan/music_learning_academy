Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, profile details, and any content you upload (recordings, assignments, practice logs).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the platform
  • To process payments and subscriptions
  • To send notifications about sessions, assignments, and announcements
  • To track learning progress and generate certificates

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data is shared only with your academy's administrators and instructors as needed for the educational service. Payment data is processed by Stripe.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request data export or account deletion at any time through your profile settings.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure password hashing, and access controls.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Contact your academy administrator or use the self-service options in your profile.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or platform notification.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact your academy administrator.
