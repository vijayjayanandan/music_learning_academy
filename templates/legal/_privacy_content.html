Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account, enroll in courses, or use our platform:

  • Account information: Name, email address, username, password, date of birth, timezone, and profile photo
  • Educational content: Practice logs, assignment submissions, recordings, and progress data
  • Payment information: Billing details processed securely by Stripe (we do not store card numbers)
  • Communications: Messages sent through the platform, session chat, and notification preferences
  • Session data: Live video/audio session recordings, attendance records, and session metadata
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and access logs (collected automatically)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the educational platform
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send notifications about sessions, assignments, and announcements
  • To track learning progress and generate certificates
  • To enable communication between students, instructors, and academy administrators
  • To improve the platform and fix technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Sub-Processors

We do not sell your personal data. Your data may be shared with:

  • Your academy's administrators and instructors — to deliver the educational service, manage enrollments, and monitor quality. Academy administrators may access session recordings for quality assurance and student safety purposes.
  • Stripe (stripe.com/privacy) — payment processing
  • SendGrid / Twilio (twilio.com/legal/privacy) — transactional email delivery
  • LiveKit Cloud (livekit.io/privacy) — live video/audio sessions, music-optimized WebRTC
  • Cloudflare R2 (cloudflare.com/privacypolicy) — file storage for recordings, uploads, and media
  • Sentry (sentry.io/privacy) — error monitoring (no personally identifiable information sent)

Each sub-processor is bound by data processing agreements and processes data only as necessary to provide their service.

4. Session Recordings

Live sessions may be recorded for educational purposes. When a session is being recorded:

  • All participants are notified before recording begins
  • Recordings are stored securely in Cloudflare R2 with tenant-scoped access
  • Academy administrators and the session instructor can access recordings
  • Recordings are used for lesson review, quality assurance, and student safety
  • You may request deletion of recordings that include you by contacting your academy administrator

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained for the duration of your account
  • Deleted accounts: All personal data and associated files are permanently deleted upon account deletion, including uploaded recordings, submissions, and profile data
  • Session recordings: Retained according to your academy's policy (default: 90 days unless flagged for retention)
  • Payment records: Transaction records retained for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting regulations
  • Server logs: Automatically purged after 30 days

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR Article 8)

We take children's privacy seriously:

  • Users under 13 (US — COPPA): Require verifiable parental consent before account creation. A parent or guardian must approve the account via email verification before the child can access the platform.
  • Users under 16 (EU — GDPR Article 8): Require parental consent for data processing. The same parental verification flow applies.
  • We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the educational service
  • Parents/guardians can review, export, or delete their child's data at any time through the parent dashboard
  • Children's data is never used for marketing or shared with third parties beyond the sub-processors listed above

7. Cookies

We use cookies as follows:

  • Essential cookies (always active): Session management, CSRF protection, and authentication. These are required for the platform to function.
  • Preference cookies (with consent): Remembering your display preferences and language settings.

We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (PBKDF2 with SHA-256)
  • Role-based access controls and tenant isolation
  • Security headers (HSTS, X-Frame-Options, Content-Type-Options)
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints
  • File upload validation (type and size restrictions)

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data (export from your profile)
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data (edit your profile)
  • Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data (delete your account)
  • Data portability: Export your data in machine-readable JSON format
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing at any time
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, use the self-service options in your profile or contact your academy administrator.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms:

  • We will notify the relevant data protection authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • We will notify affected users without undue delay via email and in-app notification
  • Notification will include: the nature of the breach, likely consequences, and measures taken or proposed

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries where our sub-processors operate. All transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email and in-app notification at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of the platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights:

  • Contact your academy administrator directly
  • Email: privacy@onemusicapp.com
